IEEE LaunchPad Kerala 2024

Official Newsletter Recap by IEEE Kerala Section & GTech MuLearn

📌 Introduction

IEEE LaunchPad Kerala is a premier job fair jointly organized by IEEE Kerala Section and GTech MuLearn. It connects top engineering talent with innovative companies across Kerala, fostering job opportunities and nurturing the startup ecosystem.

📣 Latest Updates

  • ✅ Over 12,000 students registered
  • 🏢 80+ companies participated
  • 🎯 Skill-based shortlisting powered by MuLearn
  • 📅 Event successfully held across Trivandrum, Kochi & Kozhikode

🗺️ Event Roadmap

The 2024 edition featured a rigorous assessment process through online tests and technical challenges. Shortlisted students attended interviews at their chosen locations – Trivandrum, Kochi, or Kozhikode – making the process accessible and inclusive.
