IEEE LaunchPad Kerala 2024

Official Newsletter Recap by IEEE Kerala Section & GTech MuLearn

📌 Introduction

IEEE LaunchPad Kerala 2024 was a landmark initiative jointly organized by the IEEE Kerala Section and GTech MuLearn Foundation. Designed as a state-wide career acceleration platform, LaunchPad aimed to bridge the gap between aspiring engineering talent and top-tier companies in the tech and innovation ecosystem. Through this initiative, students, fresh graduates, and early professionals were offered a golden opportunity to demonstrate their skills and connect directly with industry leaders who are actively seeking fresh, capable minds.

Unlike conventional job fairs, LaunchPad Kerala emphasized skill-based shortlisting, ensuring that candidates were evaluated on real capabilities rather than just academic scores. By integrating MuLearn’s personalized learning journeys and microlearning tracks, the event empowered participants to build confidence, strengthen core competencies, and become job-ready.

The 2024 edition attracted overwhelming participation across Kerala, setting a new benchmark in collaborative talent development. With a mission to foster employability, innovation, and inclusive hiring, LaunchPad Kerala continues to stand as a beacon of hope for young professionals seeking meaningful careers in a rapidly evolving tech landscape.

📣 Latest Updates

  • ✅ Over 12,000 students registered
  • 🏢 80+ companies participated
  • 🎯 Skill-based shortlisting powered by MuLearn
  • 📅 Event successfully held across Trivandrum, Kochi & Kozhikode

🗺️ Event Roadmap

The 2024 edition featured a rigorous assessment process through online tests and technical challenges. Shortlisted students attended interviews at their chosen locations – Trivandrum, Kochi, or Kozhikode – making the process accessible and inclusive.
